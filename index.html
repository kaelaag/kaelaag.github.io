About us

As a growing activist organization, the Moo-vement seeks to inform society of the neglected issues within animal agriculture, health, and the environment while expressing how we can improve them.

Our Mission
We are a group of passionate individuals with a common goal: Reducing society's dependence on beef. Explore the rest of our website to learn more about how you can make a difference.
Our mission is to review the current practices of animal agriculture in the United States of America and how it affects the environment, human health, economy and overall animal welfare.
